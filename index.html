The Gym

More Than a Gym —
A Lifestyle

Est. New Delhi

The Global Fitness is located just steps away from your home or place of work, we are in Vikaspuri, Shalimar Bagh, Kamla Nagar and Pitampura. Our team includes trained, energetic and enthusiastic fitness professionals whose goal is to help you improve your health and fitness.

We provide a hygienic, safe and state-of-the-art facility for our members — whether they are just starting an exercise program or are at an advanced level. The Global Fitness offers Personal Training, Bodybuilding, Cardio Equipment, Free Weights, Strength Biomechanics and Group Fitness Classes.

Good health means a complete physical, mental, social and spiritual wellbeing. The only way to achieve a fit body and mind is to remain consistent with a healthy lifestyle.

Technology & Facilities

Smart Gym
Features

We combine cutting-edge fitness technology with premium facilities to give every member the best possible experience.

🔬
BCA Machine
A complete Body Composition Analysis machine providing detailed BMI, muscle mass, and fat percentage analysis. Members can view their full history from anywhere via our website — keeping track of real fitness progress over time.
📋
Digital Schedule Cards
Electronically accessible personalised schedule cards — a six-day-per-week plan customized for your body type and fitness goals. Access from anywhere in the world through our website on any device.
🔐
Fingerprint Access System
Hassle-free entry and exit controlled by a fingerprint access system — automatically marking attendance and tracking time spent in the gym. Renewals are updated in real time for uninterrupted access.
🔒
Secure Lockers
Safe, secure and simple locker service to safely store all your valuable belongings while you train. Your gear stays protected so you can focus entirely on your workout.

Amenities

Functional Training

Functional training adds a fun, dynamic element to your workout, helping break the boredom and monotony of regular strength training. Through activities such as Monkey Ladders, Agility Ladders, Tyre Flips, Battle Ropes, BOSU Ball exercises and Stability Ball training, you build strength, endurance, balance, coordination and overall athletic performance in an engaging way.

🏢
Group Activity Studio

A dedicated studio for group classes and fun member events — building a sense of community that goes beyond exercise. We conduct Aerobic’s, Bollywood dance, Bhangra, Yoga and Power Yoga helping you with fitness without straining or risking any injuries

Ample Parking space
